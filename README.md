# DuoPoker AI

> Your AI poker coach that guides you hand by hand

## Overview
DuoPoker AI is an AI-powered poker coaching platform designed to help players of all levels master Texas Hold'em. Available at duopoker.ai, the platform delivers personalized, hand-by-hand guidance so users can improve their decision-making, strategy, and game sense at their own pace.

## How It Works
Built with a modern React + TypeScript stack using Vite and shadcn/ui components, DuoPoker AI presents players with interactive poker spots — real in-game scenarios where the AI coach analyzes choices and provides instant feedback. Players earn XP, complete daily missions, and climb leagues, making the learning experience feel more like a game than a grind. Authentication is handled via Lovable Cloud Auth, and the UI is crafted with Radix UI primitives and styled with Tailwind CSS for a polished, responsive experience.

## Use Cases
- **Beginner players** looking to learn Texas Hold'em fundamentals through guided, low-pressure scenarios
- **Intermediate players** wanting to identify and patch leaks in their preflop and postflop play
- **Competitive players** who want structured daily training routines with measurable progress through leagues and missions
- **Poker study groups** using the platform as a shared training tool with gamified accountability

With its Spanish-language interface targeting the Latin American market and a focus on gamified progression, DuoPoker AI bridges the gap between complex GTO theory and accessible, engaging daily practice.

## FAQ

### How does DuoPoker AI help me improve at Texas Hold'em?
DuoPoker AI presents you with real in-game scenarios called spots and provides instant AI-driven feedback on your decisions. Over time, this guided practice helps you internalize correct strategy and fix costly leaks in your game.

### Is DuoPoker AI suitable for complete poker beginners?
Yes, DuoPoker AI is designed for all skill levels. Beginners can learn fundamental concepts through structured, low-pressure interactive training, while the AI coach explains the reasoning behind each recommended action.

### What makes DuoPoker AI different from other poker training sites?
DuoPoker AI combines AI-powered personalized coaching with gamification features like daily missions, XP, and competitive leagues. This makes learning feel engaging rather than tedious, leading to more consistent daily practice.

### Does DuoPoker AI offer daily training challenges?
Yes, the platform includes daily missions that keep your training consistent and goal-oriented. Completing missions earns rewards and helps you track progress over time.

### Is DuoPoker AI available in Spanish?
Yes, DuoPoker AI's interface is in Spanish, making it especially accessible for players in Latin America and Spain who want to learn poker in their native language.

### Can I track my poker improvement progress on DuoPoker AI?
Absolutely. DuoPoker AI features a gamified progression system with XP, leagues, and mission completions that give you a clear picture of how your skills are developing over time.
